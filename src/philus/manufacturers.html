Cost-Effective Next-Generation Patient Access Solutions

PhilRx Patient Access Platform puts the manufacturer in control of their distribution channels.

Better Access,
Lower Costs

Unlike expensive hub models that use call-centers enabled by inflexible 3rd-party software, Phil offers a full suite of customizable patient/prescriber/pharmacy user experience, combined with full-spectrum real-time data. Powered primarily by technology, the PhilRx Platform is 10x-15x cheaper and 2x-3x more effective compared to traditional hub platforms.

  • Customizable Adjudication and Fulfillment
    +

    Customizable Script Adjudication ensures that your business rules are applied at the point of adjudication with far more flexibility than traditional copay coupons. Drive higher coverage and Customize Prior Authorization process, optimizing for profitability and patient experience, while meeting all payer requirements.

  • Nationwide Pharmacy Network
    +

    Gain access to a nationwide closed pharmacy network that implements all of your business rules with software audit mechanisms in place and real-time data feedback. Consistent, reproducible and predictable patient/prescriber experience across the entire network.

  • Modern Digital Experience
    +

    Patients experience a modern technology-driven experience that delivers lowest cost, predictable delivery and convenience. Prescribers enjoy a 360° view of the flight of the script. Real-time text and mobile communication channels for prescribers, pharmacists and patients.

  • Full Service Prior Authorization
    +

    Our flexible coverage management allows you to customize the Prior Authorization process, alleviating workflow burden for prescribers and pharmacies, while keeping patients informed.

  • Unprecedented Data
    +

    Obtain fine-grained data for every step of the workflow from prescription to refill. Gain actionable insights about patients and prescriber behaviors.

  • Wholesale/3PL capabilities
    +

    We customize logistics solutions to meet your business needs, including wholesale and inventory distribution strategy, Rx sampling, and consignment.
